# Inspectly Product Overview

classification: approved
visibility: public

Inspectly is an Analytos product for regulated inspection workflows in healthcare, labs, and field service teams.

## Features

- Evidence capture for photos, checklist responses, timestamps, and reviewer notes.
- Risk scoring that highlights inspection items most likely to fail compliance review.
- Corrective-action tracking with ownership, due dates, and escalation signals.
- Audit-ready history that shows what changed, who approved it, and why.

## Proof points

- A medical device quality team cut audit preparation time by 41%.
- Inspectly improved corrective-action closure within SLA from 72% to 91%.
- Risk scoring helped reviewers focus on the 15% of findings most likely to trigger rework.

## Target users

- Quality Director
- Compliance Manager
- Field Inspection Lead
- Operations Excellence Lead

## Positioning

Inspectly turns scattered inspection evidence into a governed operating layer for audit readiness.
