# Stockly Product Overview

classification: approved
visibility: public

Stockly is an Analytos product for retail and CPG operators that need reliable inventory decisions across stores, warehouses, and marketplaces.

## Features

- Real-time inventory reconciliation across POS, ERP, warehouse, and marketplace feeds.
- Shelf gap detection that flags likely out-of-stock conditions before the next cycle count.
- Supplier delay forecasting using purchase-order history and route-level lead-time signals.
- Store manager action queue that converts inventory anomalies into prioritized tasks.

## Proof points

- A six-store pilot reduced phantom inventory by 18% in eight weeks.
- Replenishment planners cut manual exception review time by 32%.
- Stockly reached 94% precision on shelf gap alerts during the pilot.

## Target users

- VP of Operations
- Director of Inventory Planning
- Store Operations Manager
- Supply Chain Analytics Lead

## Positioning

Stockly replaces spreadsheet-driven inventory exception triage with governed, explainable inventory intelligence.
