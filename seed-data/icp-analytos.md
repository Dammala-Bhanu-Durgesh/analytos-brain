# Analytos Ideal Customer Profile

classification: approved
visibility: public

Analytos sells governed operational intelligence products to mid-market and enterprise teams that already have data but cannot reliably turn it into daily action.

## ICP segments

### Retail Operations

- Company size: 500 to 10,000 employees.
- Stack: POS, ERP, WMS, ecommerce marketplace feeds, BI dashboards.
- Triggers: chronic stockouts, margin leakage, failed inventory transformation programs, regional expansion.
- Competitors displaced: spreadsheets, generic BI dashboards, manual exception queues.
- Best products: Stockly.

### Regulated Inspection Operations

- Company size: 300 to 20,000 employees.
- Stack: QMS, ticketing, document management, inspection checklists.
- Triggers: audit pressure, rising corrective-action backlog, repeated compliance findings, multi-site standardization.
- Competitors displaced: paper checklists, disconnected QMS exports, shared folders.
- Best products: Inspectly.

## Personas

- VP of Operations: owns operational throughput, cost reduction, and process reliability.
- Director of Inventory Planning: owns inventory accuracy, replenishment exceptions, and service levels.
- Quality Director: owns audit readiness, non-conformance reduction, and corrective-action governance.
- Compliance Manager: owns inspection evidence, traceability, and policy adherence.
